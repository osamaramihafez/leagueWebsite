What is the MAA?

The Muslim Athletic Association is a safe space for youth to learn from and engage with their communities through sport. There are many lessons and values that we can take from sport. Whether it be humility, determination, awareness, passion... the list goes on. We hope to hone these traits and many more to help build stronger muslim, both physically and mentally.

Vision

The MAA hopes to establish a community atmosphere that centres around values that come from Islam and can be developed through sport.

Does it work?

You tell us. Some of the most influential muslims are those who are involved in sports. It's only through respecting their Islamic values that these greats are respected by the masses.
