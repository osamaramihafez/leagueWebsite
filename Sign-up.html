    Team registration

    Notice: Each team must contain atleast 7 players. The maximum for each team is currently held at 10 players. Each team must have a captain who registers their team. The captain will then receive a passcode containing 6 characters. Each player on the team must then register using this passcode in order to play for that team. Please see the team rules and regulations before proceeding.

    Player Registration

    Notice: If you have not already read the above notice, please read it before you continue. Each player may only register and play on one team. Once a player has stepped onto the field of play for a team, they may no longer play for another team. Any teams caught with a player who is not registered on their team will be penalized. Once a captain creates a team, the captain will not need to register as a player since this will be done automatically. Please see the player rules and regulations before proceeding.
