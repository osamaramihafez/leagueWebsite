WHAT IS THE MAA SOCCER LEAGUE?

A league organized by Muslims for the Muslim community. Everyone is welcome.

  • Number of teams: 16
  • Field Size: 8v8
  • Roster: 12 Players; up to 14 max per team.
  • Number of Games: 18 games guarenteed (15 regular Session + 4 Cup games)
  • Every team will play 2 games per session, 40 min each - 10 mins break between games
  • First Day: January 11, 2020. (Every Saturday)
  • Games will be professionally officiated (Referees)
  • Three MAA Supervisors will be available on fields
  • Uniforms will be included (12 only)
  • An amount will be donated on behalf of all the Players, Staff, Sponsors, and Organizers to a Charity Project
  • Awards & Prizes will be presented during the closing ceremony
  • Closing Ceremony will be held at an event hall nd dinner will be included for the players (There will be a fee for Family and Friends to attend)
  • Games will be held at the Mississauga Stadium Dome, a FIFA certified Turf Field
  • Game time is between 10:30 PM and 1:30 PM
